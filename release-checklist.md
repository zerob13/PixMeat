# Release Checklist

- Launch packaged app.
- Confirm engine health shows ready.
- Open JPG and PNG portraits.
- Adjust every liquify, skin, and beauty slider.
- Verify before, split, and after compare modes.
- Export JPEG and PNG.
- Cancel an export and confirm app remains usable.
- Switch backend preference and confirm CPU fallback works.
- Clear cache manually during QA.
